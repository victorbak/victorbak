Who Am I?

I am a software developer with a passion for web, mobile, and game development, who graduated from the Computer Systems Technology program at the British Columbia Institute of Technology.

Through my studies, I found I was most interested in full-stack development. I love the idea of being able to handle databases and logic and while also designing layouts for sites and stylizing how data is displayed.

I like to code things from scratch and bring ideas to life. I strive to write clean and scalable solutions to everyday problems. My natural curiousity pushes me towards learning new techniques everyday, one problem at a time.
